How do I place an order?

Browse the menu, add items to cart, and tap 'Proceed to Pay' at checkout.

How can I track my order?

After placing an order, go to 'Orders' tab to see real-time status updates.

Can I modify my order after placing?

Once an order is placed, please speak to a staff member for modifications.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept counter payment (cash/card) and online payment at checkout.

How do I call for waiter assistance?

Use the 'Call Staff' button below or approach any of our staff members.
